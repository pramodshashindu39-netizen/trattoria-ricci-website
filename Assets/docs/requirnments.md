# Requirement Analysis - Trattoria Ricci

## 1. Chosen Client Brief
* **Client Name:** Giovanni & Elena Ricci
* **Business Type:** Family Italian Restaurant (3rd Generation)

## 2. Derived Pages and Sections
* **Home Page (`index.html`):** Welcome text, Hero image section, and a Call-to-Action button to book a table.
* **Our Story (`about.html`):** 3-generation family history, Grandfather's photo layout, and heritage storytelling.
* **Menu (`menu.html`):** Categorized list with prices spanning 5 specific sections: Starters, Pasta, Mains, Desserts, and Wine list.
* **Private Events (`events.html`):** Dedicated details about hosting birthdays and anniversaries in the exclusive back room.
* **Reservations & Contact (`contact.html`):** Opening hours, contact information, and a frontend booking HTML form.

## 3. Design Direction & Justification
* **Mood:** Warm, cozy, and deeply welcoming (matching a traditional family restaurant style).
* **Color Palette:** Warm tones (Deep Wine Red `#8B0000`, Gold `#FFD700`, and Cream `#FFFDD0`) to mimic warm restaurant lighting. Cold corporate colors have been strictly avoided.
* **Typography:** Classic Serif font (`Georgia`) used for headings to preserve an authentic, heritage, and high-quality feel.
* **Imagery:** Structured to support authentic food and interior photography instead of generic stock images.

## 4. Engineering Assumptions
* The reservation form is purely front-end (HTML/CSS) and does not store data in a database.
* Opening hours are set statically but can be configured dynamically in future phases.
