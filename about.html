The Ricci Family Legacy

Our founder, Nonno Ricci, in his original kitchen (1960).

Three Generations of Passion

Our story began over 60 years ago when our grandfather brought his secret family recipes from Sicily. He believed that good food brings people together like nothing else.

Today, Giovanni and Elena Ricci continue this beautiful tradition. Every pasta strand is rolled by hand, and every sauce is simmered for hours, keeping the authentic taste alive for your family to enjoy.
